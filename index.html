Prevention of DDoS attack in SDN [SUNY Binghamton,Oct 2018 – Dec 2018]

Distributed denial of service (DDoS) attacks in cloud computing environments are growing due to the essential characteristics of cloud computing. With recent advances in software-defined networking (SDN), SDN-based cloud brings us new chances to defeat DDoS attacks in cloud computing environments. The capabilities of SDN, including software-based traffic analysis, centralized control, global view of the network, and dynamic updating of forwarding rules, make it easier to detect and react to DDoS attacks. Although central control is the major advantage of SDN, it is also a single point of failure if it is made unreachable by a Distributed Denial of Service Attack. Two main objectives of this project are utilizing the central control of SDN for attack detection and, proposing a solution that is effective and lightweight in terms of the resources that it uses. This project provides a solution to detect such attacks based on the entropy variation of destination IP address and flow initiation rate.
Technology: Python, mininet, pox controller [Github]

Rank based Document Search

I have designed an algorithm for efficiently searching a document from the group of documents based on user-given search input. I have also considered various privacy level of those documents to be shown to other friends of the user. The customized interface of the system stores documents content in XML format. Whenever a user searches for a keyword, that keyword might be present in many of his documents. I had to prioritize the document order in which it would get displayed to the user. Once a document is created, content is extracted from its corresponding XML tags. After processing and refining, stop words are eliminated from the content set as I try to capture significant and meaningful words along with its priority value and frequency count. From rest of the content, I have segregated proper noun and common noun for setting priority value accordingly. The priority of words for each document is measured and defined by the various factor.
a> The word importance type- whether it is bold, italic, underlined, colored or other way marked. That information is captured from XML Tag
b> whether the word is proper noun or common noun
c> whether the word is valid English Dictionary word
d> Word density within the document
When the user searches for a document by giving a keyword, that keyword is checked in the database for fetching priority value and the corresponding document link. The relevant document links having the higher priority value are shown to user.
Technology: C#,HTML, Java Script
